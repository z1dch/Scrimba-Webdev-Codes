Polytechnic University of the Philippines

The Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) is a premier state university dedicated to providing accessible, high-quality, and socially relevant polytechnic education. Guided by its core philosophy of “Mula Sa'Yo, Para sa Bayan", the institution serves as a powerful socioeconomic equalizer by offering comprehensive programs in engineering, business, technology, and the social sciences to a diverse student body. Through its main campus in Manila and an extensive network of branches nationwide, PUP fosters academic excellence, civic responsibility, and institutional resilience, consistently developing highly skilled professionals equipped to contribute to national development.
